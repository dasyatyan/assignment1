Our hikes involve walking, requiring no prior skills. You'll learn camping essentials like tent setup, fire-making, and storytelling along the way. No special equipment is needed; we follow mountain trails. While it may initially feel challenging, you'll adapt quickly to the camping lifestyle and discover newfound strength within a day.

You're capable! We closely monitor hikers' conditions, adjust the pace to their abilities, and maintain a flexible hiking plan. While you'll need to exert effort and sweat, if you're prepared and have no medical issues, you're welcome to join us in the mountains!

Common participants in such trips are city dwellers seeking a nature escape but lacking experience or companions. Typically, they work in offices, use the Internet, reside in urban areas, appreciate nature, and are generally well-intentioned. Hiking tends to attract individuals with positive interests.

For one-day summer trips, children as young as 7 years old can safely participate, depending on their behavior and health. Children often adapt well to camping conditions with a good example to follow. Starting with shorter hikes near your city is a good way to gauge their interest before embarking on longer journeys.
